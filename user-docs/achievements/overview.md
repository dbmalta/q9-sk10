# Achievements Overview

The Achievements module tracks badges, awards, and training records for your members.

## Concepts

**Achievement definitions** are the types of awards available in your organisation — e.g. "Scout Badge", "First Aid Training", "Wood Badge".

**Awards** are instances of an achievement granted to a specific member on a specific date.

## Workflow

1. An administrator creates achievement definitions (done once per badge/award type)
2. Leaders assign those achievements to individual members as they are earned
