# Settings

System-wide settings are in **Admin → Settings**.

## Organisation

- **Organisation name** — shown in the header and emails
- **Country** — used for date and address formatting
- **Logo** — upload your organisation's logo

## Email

- **SMTP host, port, username, password** — for outgoing email
- **From address** and **From name** — shown on outgoing emails

## Registration

- **Public registration** — enable/disable the public waiting list form
- **Registration sections** — which sections appear in the registration form

## Custom fields

Add and manage custom member fields. See [Custom Fields](../members/custom-fields.md).

## Terms & Conditions

Upload or paste the T&C text that members must accept during registration.

## Notices

Create system-wide notices shown to all logged-in users (e.g. "System maintenance on Friday").
