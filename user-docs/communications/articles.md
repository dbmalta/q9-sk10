# Articles

Articles are posts that appear in the internal news/notice board visible to logged-in users.

## Creating an article

1. Go to **Communications → Articles → New Article**
2. Enter a title and body content
3. Set the publication date (defaults to now)
4. Choose visibility (all members, or specific sections)
5. Save as **Draft** or **Publish** immediately

## Managing articles

The articles list shows all articles with their status. Published articles are visible to members; drafts are only visible to staff.

To unpublish an article, open it and change its status back to **Draft**.
