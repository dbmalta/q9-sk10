# Managing Members

The Members module is the core of ScoutKeeper. It stores all member records for your organisation.

## Member list

Navigate to **Members** in the sidebar to see the full member list. You can:

- **Search** by name, email, or membership number
- **Filter** by section, status, or custom fields
- **Sort** by any column

## Member record

Each member has:

- **Basic details** — name, date of birth, contact information
- **Membership details** — join date, section assignment, membership status
- **Custom fields** — any additional fields your organisation has configured
- **Timeline** — a log of events and notes on the member's record
- **Attachments** — uploaded documents (e.g. consent forms)
- **Achievements** — badges and training records

## Member statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Active | Current member |
| Inactive | Former member, record retained |
| Waiting | On the waiting list |
