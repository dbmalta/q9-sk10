# Waiting List

The waiting list holds prospective members who have expressed interest but cannot yet be accommodated.

## How it works

Waiting list entries are a lightweight record containing contact details and the section applied for. They do not count as full member records and do not appear in member reports.

## Adding to the waiting list

Go to **Members → Waiting List → Add Entry**. Enter the applicant's name, contact details, and the section they are interested in.

## Converting to a member

When a place becomes available:

1. Open the waiting list entry
2. Click **Convert to Member**
3. The form pre-fills with the existing details — complete any missing fields and save

The waiting list entry is removed once conversion is confirmed.

## Public registration

If your administrator has enabled public registration, prospective members can add themselves to the waiting list via a public URL. See [Member Registration](registration.md).
